Why I built Allotment Hub

I created Allotment Hub to bring together everything I’ve learnt on my own plot into one place. After years of scribbling notes about seed timings, swapping seedlings with friends and experimenting with layouts, I wanted a home for all that knowledge. This site is a living project – built to help fellow gardeners get organised, share resources and connect with a growing community.
